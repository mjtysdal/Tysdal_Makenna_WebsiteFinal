Meet the Chefs

Chefs Tim Flores and Genie Kwon took a big risk when they left the two-star Michelin rated restaurant, Oriole, to start their own in 2020. In spite of all the hardships that they have endured, Kasama has become a local favorite in the East Ukrainian Village of Chicago. Kwon and Flores give Filipino cuisine a modern twist in the fine dining arena. Both chefs are experts in flavor and technique which is displayed through each dish at Kasama, from morning till night.
